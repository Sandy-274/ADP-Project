B.Tech Mechatronics Engineering

Mechatronics Engineering is the sub-branch of engineering, which mainly focuses on the engineering of both electrical and mechanical systems. It also includes a combination of robotics, electronics, computer, and many other elements that are included in the course. This course on Mechatronics Engineering is very useful as it helps the student study/know more about robotics. The market/consumers these days have high demands resulting in the innovation of more complex products, and in that way, mechatronics engineering is used to integrate complex, electrical and software components.
